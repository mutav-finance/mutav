## The Insight

To rent an apartment in Brazil, you need to find someone who loves you enough to risk their home. That is the guarantee system in the world's eighth-largest economy.

When I came across the CredPago story — a company that replaced the fiador, grew 10x in three years, and was acquired by Loft for a price that turned BTG Pactual's investment into R$1.4 billion (~$280M) in under two years — I had one question: *what comes next?*

The capital infrastructure behind these guarantees is still completely closed. CredPago, QuintoAndar, the insurance companies: they all raise their own capital, price risk asymmetrically, and capture the entire return. No participation. No transparency. A market generating ~$1.1B (R$5.4B) in annual fees across all paid guarantee modalities, inaccessible to anyone outside that circle.

That is the gap MUTAV was built to close.

---

## What MUTAV Is

A pool of capital sits behind a portfolio of rental contracts. When tenants pay, the pool grows. When tenants default, the pool covers. The structure is not novel — reinsurance protocols have used it for decades. What has never existed is this structure applied to Brazilian rental guarantees, with the capital pool open to external investors and the full execution on-chain.

MUTAV Soluções acts as institutional guarantor under Art. 37 of Brazil's Lei do Inquilinato — the same legal basis CredPago used. The difference: the capital behind the guarantees belongs to the Mutav Treasury Fund, where any KYC-cleared investor holds a proportional stake. Two return streams flow in — 80% of tenant fees and yield from tokenized Brazilian Treasury bonds via Etherfuse on Stellar. Both are incorporated directly into NAV. You hold MUTAV tokens, the tokens appreciate.

The programmability is not cosmetic. Stellar's sub-cent fees and 5-second finality make the unit economics work for retail. Etherfuse is the only institutional-grade provider of tokenized BR Treasury on any chain. When the fund covers a default, execution is deterministic — no manual steps, no waiting.
