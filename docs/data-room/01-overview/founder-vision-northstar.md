## The Insight

To rent an apartment in Brazil, you need to find someone who loves you enough to risk their home. That is the guarantee system in the world's eighth-largest economy.

When I came across the CredPago story — a startup that replaced the fiador, grew 10x in three years, and was acquired by Loft in a deal that returned R$1.4 billion (~$280M) to BTG Pactual in under two years — I had one question: *what comes next?*

The capital behind these guarantees is still completely closed. CredPago, QuintoAndar, the insurance companies: they all raise their own capital and keep the entire return. No external participation. No transparency. A market generating ~$1.1B/year in fees, inaccessible to outside investors.

That is the gap MUTAV was built to close.

---

## What MUTAV Is

A pool of capital sits behind a portfolio of rental contracts. When tenants pay, the pool grows. When tenants default, the pool covers. This structure has been used in credit and insurance for decades. What has never existed is this applied to Brazilian rental guarantees, with the capital pool open to any investor.

MUTAV Soluções acts as institutional guarantor under Art. 37 of Brazil's Lei do Inquilinato — the same legal basis CredPago used. The difference: the capital behind the guarantees belongs to the Mutav Treasury Fund, where any verified investor holds a stake. Two income streams flow in — 80% of tenant fees and yield from tokenized Brazilian Treasury bonds. Both accrue directly into the token value. You hold MUTAV tokens, the tokens appreciate.

The blockchain infrastructure is not cosmetic. Stellar's low-cost transactions and Etherfuse's tokenized Treasury bonds make the unit economics viable from the first contracts. When the fund covers a default, execution is automatic — no manual steps.

## The Structural Advantages

**Distribution:** Brazil's rental market flows through 74,000 real estate agencies. Our advisor Cinara Bigóis has 30 years of earned trust in the Sul region. The pilot is not a cold campaign — it is a warm introduction to agencies that already know us and have real problems to solve.

**Data:** Every contract generates a time-stamped payment record. Over time, that becomes the most accurate rental credit dataset in Brazil. You can only build it by being first.

Five years from now, MUTAV is the capital reserve infrastructure for rental guarantees in Brazil — the layer that agencies and investors route capital through because it is the most transparent and efficient option.

- 500,000+ rental contracts backed by MUTAV
- $500M+ under management across three risk classes (MTVH, MTVM, MTVL)
- MUTAV tokens recognized as a yield-generating asset in global crypto markets

CredPago replaced the personal guarantor. MUTAV replaces the closed capital structure behind it — and opens it to the world.

## Why Now

**Market gap:** QuintoAndar exited the guarantee segment in 2024. CredPago was absorbed by Loft. The next winner is undecided.

**Proven model:** Maple Finance, Centrifuge, and Goldfinch proved that credit instruments can be managed on blockchain. None touched Brazilian rental. MUTAV applies a validated model to a market that has never seen it.

**Regulatory tailwind:** The Extrajudicial Eviction Law (PL 3.999/20, CCJ approved June 2025) speeds up eviction timelines — making guarantees more powerful and increasing demand for the capital behind them.
