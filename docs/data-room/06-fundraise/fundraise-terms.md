## Use of Proceeds

The pre-seed round funds MUTAV to its first mainnet milestone: a live product with 600 active guarantee contracts, $100K genesis AUM, and the first fee cycle closed — generating $32K/month in fee income to the fund.

| Category | Allocation | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Fund allocation | ~50% | Capital deployed directly into the fund as genesis AUM |
| Compliance & entity constitution | ~20% | Offshore entity (Cayman/BVI/Bermuda), Brazilian legal counsel (MUTAV Soluções), Subscription Agreement, Advisor agreements |

## What Investors Are Getting

**The thesis:** MUTAV opens Brazil's closed rental guarantee capital market to external investors for the first time. The pre-seed funds the proof: live contracts, live assets, live returns.

**Entry terms:** pre-seed investors enter at **$1.00 token price** — the launch price, before any fee income or Treasury yield accrues. This is the ground floor: the model projects **5x token price and $50K/month net yield by month 18**.

**The equity story:** investors in the pre-seed are buying into the entity that owns MUTAV Soluções — the Brazilian operating company that captures 20% of all tenant fees and holds the mandatory first-loss position in the fund. As the fund grows, the value of that position grows.

**The token story:** [TBD — if token warrants are included, define the instrument here. The MUTAV token is a fund share, not a governance or utility token. Pre-seed investors may receive warrants for MTVH tokens at a discounted price.]

**The risk:** this is pre-revenue, pre-mainnet. The capital buys the time and resources to get to a live product. Core execution risks: regulatory classification, Etherfuse eligibility for the offshore fund, and pilot adoption speed. All three are being worked in parallel.

## Path to Self-Sufficiency

The fund becomes self-sustaining at a relatively low asset threshold:

| Assets / Contracts | Monthly fee income to fund | Self-sufficient? |
|---|---|---|
| $500K / ~1,000 contracts | ~$32,000/month | No — foundational operations |
| $2M / ~4,000 contracts | ~$128,000/month | Near self-sustaining |
| $5M / ~10,000 contracts | ~$320,000/month | Yes — operations + growth funded by fees |

The pre-seed does not fund MUTAV forever. It funds MUTAV to the point where the business runs on its own fee income.

## What Happens After Pre-Seed

**Seed round (~$5–6M, ~18 months post-close):** triggered by live mainnet + contract growth track record + $3M+ in assets. Used for national expansion, institutional investor access, and full three-tranche architecture.

**Series A (~36 months post-close):** triggered by national distribution, $20M+ in assets, institutional share above 20%. Goal: MUTAV as the reserve infrastructure for Brazil's guarantee capital market.
