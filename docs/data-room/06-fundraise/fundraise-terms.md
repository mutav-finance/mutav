## What Investors Are Getting

**The thesis:** MUTAV converts Brazil's closed rental guarantee capital market into an open, programmable, onchain infrastructure layer. The pre-seed funds the proof: live contracts, live AUM, live returns.

**The equity story:** investors in the pre-seed are buying into the entity that owns MUTAV Soluções (the Brazilian operational company) — which captures 20% of all tenant fees, and holds the mandatory MTVH position in the fund. As the fund grows, the value of that position grows.

**The token story:** [TBD — if token warrants are included, define the instrument here. The MUTAV token is the fund share, not a governance or utility token. Investors in the pre-seed equity round may receive warrants for MTVH tokens at a discounted NAV.]

**The risk:** this is pre-revenue, pre-mainnet. The capital buys the time and resources to get to a live product. The core execution risks are regulatory (VASP classification, L1), Etherfuse eligibility (ET1), and pilot adoption speed. All three are being worked in parallel.

## Capital Efficiency: Path to Self-Sufficiency

The model's most important property is that it becomes self-sustaining at a relatively low AUM threshold:

| AUM Level | Monthly fee income to fund (80% × contracts × R$160/contract) | Management fee revenue | Operational self-sufficiency |
|---|---|---|---|
| $500K / ~1,000 contracts | ~$32,000/month | ~$417/month (1% p.a.) | No — foundational operations |
| $2M / ~4,000 contracts | ~$128,000/month | ~$1,667/month | Near self-sustaining |
| $5M / ~10,000 contracts | ~$320,000/month | ~$4,167/month | Yes — operations + growth funded by fees |

The pre-seed does not need to fund MUTAV forever. It funds MUTAV to the point where the business runs on its own fee income.

## What Happens After Pre-Seed

**Seed round target ($3-5M, projected ~18 months post-close):**
Triggered by: live mainnet + track record of contract growth + AUM above $3M threshold.
Purpose: national expansion (beyond Sul region), institutional investor access, tranche architecture (MTVM/MTVL), DeFi integrations.

**Series A (projected ~36 months post-close):**
Triggered by: national distribution, $20M+ AUM, institutional AUM > 20%.
Purpose: MUTAV as the reserve infrastructure for Brazil's guarantee capital market.
