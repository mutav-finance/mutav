01Problem
"In 2025, QuintoCred, one of Brazil's largest rental guarantors, shut down overnight. 45,000 contracts cancelled. 3,000 agencies without coverage."
01
Concentrated capital,
systemic risk
Guarantors operate with their own isolated capital. When one fails, the entire market collapses. For the agencies left exposed, there was no recourse and no timeline for resolution.
QuintoCred, shut down 2025 · 45K contracts
CredPago, default 2025 · 16K agencies
02
Lack of transparency,
slow settlement
When the guarantee is triggered, what follows are days of bureaucracy and little clarity around deadlines and required documentation. Meanwhile, tenants pay 10–15% per month in fees, with no transparency regarding where that capital goes.
03
A billion-reais market
with no entry point
An investor watching BTG Pactual generate billion-dollar profits in rental guarantees in under two years has no mechanism to replicate that exposure. Over $770 million flows through rental guarantee premiums annually in Brazil, and the sector is structurally closed to outside capital. No entry point.
02Solution

MUTAV opens the Brazilian rental guarantee market, distributing risk across a tokenized fund, automating settlement through smart contracts, and making real yield from rental contracts accessible to crypto investors.

Risk distributed across tokenized fund pools, no single-guarantor failure point
Smart contract automation: clear timelines, full transparency, fast default settlement
Tokenized RWA fund: any crypto investor enters from $1K, earning real yield from Brazilian rental contracts

05How It Works
01. AGENCIES
Smart Contract via Dashboard
Each real estate agency joins the protocol through a dedicated dashboard and receives an individual smart contract. Tenant and landlord contracts are registered inside it.

02. RISK ENGINE
Scoring & Fund Grouping
Every contract receives a risk score. The agency's score is the weighted average of all active contracts. The protocol groups agencies into risk-tiered funds, enabling granular risk management at scale.

06Business Model

B2B2C: the B2B side integrates real estate agencies as guarantee infrastructure; the B2C side operates as an RWA fund tokenization protocol. MUTAV retains 20% of the guarantee fee as protocol spread, the remaining 80% flows to the investor fund as yield.
