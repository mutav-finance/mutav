## 02 · Problem

**A closed and structurally fragile market**

### 1 — Concentrated capital, systemic risk

Guarantors operate with their own capital, no outside participation. When one fails — like QuintoCred and CredPago in 2025, affecting 45,000 contracts — there's no collective absorption mechanism. The risk stays trapped in a few players.

### 2 — High cost, slow settlement

Tenants pay **10–15% per year** in guarantee fees. Landlords wait **up to 60 days** to receive payment on defaults, dependent on manual and bureaucratic processes with no predictability.

### 3 — Market inaccessible to investors

A sector moving **billions in annual premiums** remains closed to individuals and the crypto ecosystem. No mechanism exists for outside participation in the returns generated by guarantees.

## 03 · Solution

### What is TGA

**TGA (Token de Garantia de Aluguel — Rental Guarantee Token)** is an onchain infrastructure protocol that turns the Brazilian rental guarantee market into a real-yield asset accessible globally.

The protocol operates on two simultaneous fronts:

- **B2B — for the traditional market:** integrates real estate agencies, offering onchain infrastructure for registration, management, and automatic settlement of guarantee contracts. Replaces manual processes without changing existing commercial dynamics.
- **B2C — for investors:** offers participation in a real assets fund by issuing a digital asset representing a collateralized stake in the **TGA SA (Segregated Account)** — a structure where investor capital is segregated from the protocol and allocated exclusively to rental guarantee coverage, collecting fees from the rental guarantee market and generating yield.

### Who it's for

| Profile | Proposition |
|---|---|
| **Crypto-native investor** | RWA exposure with real yield; diversification into a Brazilian real estate asset |
| **Institutional investor** | Structured fund with onchain-verifiable collateral and yield from real assets |
| **Real estate agencies** | Guarantee automation, operational efficiency, 1% fee per contract | benefits for their **Landlords**, offering automatic settlement in days, not months | benefits for their **Tenants** offering easier rental access; simpler and potentially cheaper process

### Positioning

> The only onchain protocol with direct exposure to the Brazilian rental guarantee market. Doesn't compete with real estate agencies — operates as the infrastructure layer connecting them to global capital and better guarantee terms.

---

### How it works — investor flow

**Appreciating NAV model:** returns aren't distributed, they're built into the fund. Fees from active contracts + collateral yield continuously raise the value of each token.

| Step | Mechanics |
|---|---|
| **Entry (Mint)** | Investor deposits stablecoin → protocol calculates current TGA SA NAV → issues proportional TGA tokens |
| **Yield** | Fees paid by tenants on active contracts + yield management generates stablecoin collateral yield → NAV rises daily |
| **Exit (Redeem)** | Investor redeems on the platform → enters onchain queue → receives stablecoin at NAV at the time of execution |

**Fund structure:**
- The fund available for investment is categorized by region/agency and risk level, which directly affects yield conditions for the investor.
- The fund has a 20% layer to provide liquidity.

---

### How it works — technical infrastructure

**SGR (Registered Guarantee System)**
Each guarantee contract is registered onchain via smart contracts. The agency accesses it via dashboard and registers the contract, which then exists as an immutable record on the blockchain. The fund's collateral is publicly auditable in real time, without exposing sensitive data, with performance verifiable by region/agency.

**Programmable Settlement Layer**
When there's a default, an offchain integration triggers the responsible smart contract, which validates the contract conditions and automatically executes settlement. The payment is directed to the agency, which forwards it to the landlord. Reduces execution time from up to 60 days to a matter of days.

## 04 · Business Model

The protocol operates on a B2B2C model — the B2B side works with real estate agencies in the traditional market as a rental guarantee provider; the B2C side operates as an RWA investment fund tokenization protocol. The project captures value through four mechanisms:

| Revenue Stream | Description |
|---|---|
| **Fee spread** | Difference between the fee charged to the tenant and the % allocated to the fund |
| **Management fee** | Fee on TGA SA AUM | Yield generation targets |
| **Redemption fee** | Charged on the amount withdrawn at investor exit |
| **NAV appreciation** | Returns built into the fund via fees + collateral yield |

### Conservative projection

- Brazilian digital guarantee market: **R$ 300M/year (2025)**
- Estimated initial penetration: **20%** of the market
- **2% spread** on intermediated volume

> **R$ 1.2M/year** in estimated revenue in the initial phase — conservative projection based exclusively on fee spread, excluding management fee, redemption fee, or NAV appreciation.
