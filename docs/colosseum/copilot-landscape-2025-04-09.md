## The Gap

Nobody has built rent default insurance (seguro fianca) on Solana. Based on the available data, this specific product — where a landlord or tenant pays a premium to a pool that covers missed rent — doesn't exist in the Solana ecosystem.

## Archive Insights

- **Placeholder VC on systemic risk:** Insurance underwriters can promote best practices — aligns with using premium pricing to incentivize good tenant behavior.
- **Drift's revenue pool model:** Insurance fund staking with cooldown periods — a pattern adaptable for the risk pool.
- **Galaxy Research on DeFi risk frameworks:** Insurance funds with socialized loss mechanisms and transparent coverage documentation.
- **Nick Szabo's "Smart Liens":** Early thinking on using smart contracts for property-related guarantees and credit risk.

---

## Why This Is Interesting

- **Real problem:** Seguro fianca in Brazil is ~8-12% of annual rent, slow claims, opaque. Porto Seguro, Tokio Marine dominate — ripe for disruption.
- **Clear on-chain primitives:** Escrow for rent, risk pools for coverage, oracles for payment verification, stablecoins (USDC / BRL stablecoins) for settlement.
- **No competition on Solana:** Rental platforms exist but none offer the insurance layer.
- **Wedge:** Start with Brazil (known market), expand to other LatAm countries with similar models.
